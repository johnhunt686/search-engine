Crabulon is a Pre-Hardmode boss, intended to be fought after the Eye of Cthulhu.

Spawn
Crabulon does not spawn naturally and the player is required to summon it by using the Decapodita Sprout in the Glowing Mushroom biome at any time.

Behavior
Crabulon will begin walking around and jumping at the player, all while firing a series of mushroom projectiles. It also starts summoning small Crab Shroom minions that slowly float downward while drifting towards the player. Crabulon will stay in its walking phase for a shorter time if it gets hit or the player is too close to it.

Attacks
Every 5.375 seconds while idling, Crabulon fires 1 Mushroom Shot at the player, which is affected by gravity.
Mushroom shots start out translucent when fired and will not deal damage until they start falling, indicated by a round flash of particles. (This also applies to the falling mushrooms in higher difficulties)
After 6 seconds of idling, it repeatedly slams its claws around. During this animation, it jumps towards the player 3 times, each with a 5-second delay.
When 5% of its health is lost, the resulting hit launches 2 / 3  / 4  Crab Shrooms upwards.
Crab Shrooms drift back and forth around the player and have a constant falling acceleration.

Resistances
Crabulon takes 80% damage from true melee hits.
Aftermath
After Crabulon is killed, a Goblin Invasion will start if one has not already been defeated in that world.
This will happen regardless of whether any players in the world have more than 200 max health, or any Shadow Orbs/Crimson Hearts have been destroyed.
Notes
If Crabulon is stuck and cannot get to the player, it can swim through blocks, similarly to the Mourning Wood and Everscream.
The effects that Crabulon makes in the Get fixed boi secret world seed can cause ill effects in real life to people with flashing lights/motion sickness problems.
Tips
For strategies on defeating Crabulon, including weapon and arena recommendations, see Guide:Crabulon strategies.
Trivia
Crabulon's theme is 1NF3$+@+!0N, which was composed by the artist DM DOKURO.
When 1NF3$+@+!0N is played through a vectorscope, portions of the song create shapes that resemble mushrooms.
The theme's name reads "Infestation".
Crabulon is often referred to as "The Perfect One" in the player base in reference to its former lore where it was given the title.
If the Boss Checklist Mod is installed, its unique despawn message will be "The monstrous mycelial crab shuffles away..."
The Bestiary entry for the Crabulon: "A crustacean that once made its home in the heart of the Ilmeran Sea, managing to escape during the Great Incineration. Crabulon fled underground, enjoying the familiar blue glow given off by glowing mushrooms. Though it succumbed to the spores, it died in peace, reminded of its home."
